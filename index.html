Frequently asked

Why is there a price range within each tier?

Each filing is sized by candidate-wallet count, owner-record completeness, and how much remediation we need on your authorized address list. We quote at the low end for holders with clean records under 5,000 candidate wallets, and at the upper end for higher complexity.

Do you charge per wallet?

No. The complexity of the filing is what we price against — not the raw wallet count. A holder with 50,000 wallets but only 200 candidates pays the same as a holder with 5,000 wallets and 200 candidates.

Is the tool license included or separate?

Included in all plans. We don't sell software-only licenses — Anchor Compliance is paired with services because the regulatory interpretation needs a human in the loop on attestation.

Do you offer multi-year discounts?

Yes — 10% off year two, 15% off year three for Annual Retainer customers who commit upfront.

What's your refund policy?

Pilot tier: if we don't deliver the filing artifacts to your specifications, you don't pay the final 50%. Annual tier: pro-rated refund for unused months minus delivered services.

Can we run Anchor on our own infrastructure?

On Enterprise, yes — we'll deploy to your environment. On Pilot and Annual, the tool runs on your laptop or your operator's machine; your data stays local.

Do you carry E&O insurance?

Yes. Certificate of insurance available on request to Annual and Enterprise customers.
