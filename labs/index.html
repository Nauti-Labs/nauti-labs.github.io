02 · Operating model

Simple enough to explain. Strict enough to trust.

The system language is public-friendly: AI work starts from Bridge, moves through stations, writes to the log, and stops at approval when the real world is involved.

Send crew

Bridge sends the right crew member to the right station.

Prepare work

The station reads, drafts, checks, or prepares a safe next step.

Ask approval

Anything sensitive waits in Captain's Approval or Clearance.

Log it

The Captain's Log keeps the action readable and reviewable.
