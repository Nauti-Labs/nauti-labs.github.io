Triton + Nauti-Vision

Maritime agency work runs on information — vessel ETAs, CBP forms, cargo docs, terminal updates. Most of it moves by email, phone, and memory. Triton is the system we built to change that.

It's a web-based AI command center that orchestrates a fleet of purpose-built agents. Every action goes through a human-in-the-loop approval layer — the AI proposes, you approve. Nothing moves without a captain at the helm.

Nauti-Vision is the eyes. Built on the same visual intelligence stack used in modern mobile devices, it processes live camera feeds to identify vessels, inspect cargo, and flag anomalies at the dock — in real time, without a specialist on site.

Live Product

Clearance

Clearance is the human approval API for agent commerce. If an agent needs to buy something, send something, or trigger a sensitive action, it asks for a human sign-off first.

The result is a signed clearance token that any service can verify. That gives teams a clean approval layer between human intent and automated execution instead of relying on blind trust.

Free tier is live now. Preferred paid settlement is USDC on Base, with any non-default asset handled only through invoice-confirmed instructions priced to a USD-equivalent amount.
